# STEEL SURGE

**Arcade RTS | 5–15 min matches | Low APM | PvP & Co-op**

Steel Surge is a fast-paced arcade real-time strategy game where the classic RTS formula meets streamlined controls and relentless action. No base building, no complex tech trees — just tactical decisions, territory control, and continuous combat.

---

## 🎮 KEY FEATURES

### ⚡ One Resource — Energy
- Earn energy from captured outposts and enemy kills
- Spend instantly on unit summons and global abilities
- No wood, no gold, no mana — just tactical choices

### 🏰 Territory Control
- **Outposts** — passive income (+5 energy/sec each)
- **Generators** — 50% faster unit summoning on connected outposts
- **Arsenal** — free global ability charges for your entire team
- Active capture system: hold E at terminals, interrupted by damage

### ⚔️ Rock-Paper-Scissors Combat

### 💀 Comeback Mechanics
- **Partisan Tactics** — summon behind enemy lines when all outposts are lost
- **Sudden Death** — after 12 minutes, all outposts deactivate, free units every 20 seconds

### 🎮 Low APM Controls
- Smart unit AI — automatically use cover, don't get stuck
- Three simple orders: **Assault / Hold / Retreat**
- Summon units without moving camera — click card, click minimap
- **Tactical Slow** — 3 times per match, slow time by 80% for 3 seconds to plan
